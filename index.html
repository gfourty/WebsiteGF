Born in Toulouse, France, Guilhem has started playing music at the age of 6. He studied drums and percussions at the Toulouse conservatory of music where he found a special interest in Jazz. He also studied at the drum school Dante Agostini with Daniel Dumoulin, as well as Jean Jaurès University, specialized in Jazz performance. He received a scholarship to attend the Berklee College in Boston to study with his mentor Neal Smith as well as Greg Hopkins, Ralph Peterson, Billy Kilson, Alain Mallet, Edmar Colon, Terri Lyne Carrington, Francisco Mela, Jerry Leake,...
