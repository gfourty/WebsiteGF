Touring musician / Studio drummer / Composer / Educator

Born in Toulouse, France, Guilhem has started playing music at the age of 5. He studied drums and percussions at the Toulouse conservatory of music where he found a special interest in Jazz. He also studied at the drum school Dante Agostini with Daniel Dumoulin, as well as Jean Jaurès University, specialized in Jazz performance. At the age of 19, he received a scholarship to attend the Berklee College of Music in Boston where he had the chance to study with Neal Smith, Greg Hopkins, Ralph Peterson, Billy Kilson, Alain Mallet, Terri Lyne Carrington,... Emerging musician of the Boston scene, Guilhem has played with Miguel Zenón, Peter Eldridge (NY Voices), Bill Pierce, Ian Coury, Giorgio Albanese, Shynia Lin, Tony Malaby, and has performed in many venues around the world (France, United States, Brasil,...).

Miguel Zenon Berklee Quintet

World class saxophonist Miguel Zenon chose Berklee students to play his original music. This quintet features Miguel Zenon, Ray Logan, Naomie Nakanishi, Noam Tanzer, and Guilhem Fourty.

Beira Mar Quintet

Beira Mar performs a modern Brazilian repertoire, featuring Chella Nahas, Gabriel Maia, Sam Chandler, Nick Isherwood, and Guilhem Fourty. By mixing various Brazilian traditions with jazz and the art of improvisation, the quintet creates its own unique sound.

Liam Elion Trio

Jazz trio led by the trombonist and bass player Liam Elion. This group features Liam Elion, Ebba Dankel, and Guilhem Fourty.
